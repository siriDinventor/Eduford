About Us

Empower Her is an tech initiative meant to support women, it was created in 2019 by Elizabeth Okome, who formed the with the intention of emowering women in techgnology.On the first of July, the program was officially launched.

EmpowerHer is an NGO, founded 2019 in Benin City, Nigeria, Working at grassroots for and with women living in impoverished slums. It is staffed by a dedicated team whose shared goal is to support and empower disa dvantagedwomen and girls who are affected by poverty, discrimination,abuse and violation of their right entitlement and control of finances.
